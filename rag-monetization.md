
# Monetization — Enterprise RAG Platform
## Knowledge-as-API

---

## Product Positioning
The Enterprise RAG Platform is **AI infrastructure**, not an application.
It is monetized on **capacity, reliability, and risk reduction**, not users.

Target buyer:
**CTO / Head of AI / Platform Engineering Lead**

---

## What Customers Pay For
- Secure access to private knowledge
- Hallucination reduction & trust
- Evaluation & observability
- Cost governance
- Multi-tenant isolation

---

## Core Pricing Model

### 1. Platform Subscription (Primary)

**Base Platform Fee**
- ₹80k – ₹2.5L / month
- ($1k–$3k globally)

Includes:
- User signup / signin (JWT auth)
- Tenant isolation & RBAC
- Knowledge ingestion pipelines
- Hybrid retrieval (vector + keyword)
- RAG APIs
- Admin dashboard
- Basic SLA & support

---

### 2. Usage-Based Pricing (Additive)

Charged monthly based on:
- Embeddings generated
- Vector storage
- LLM tokens (10–25% margin)
- High-QPS access
- Additional tenants

This aligns pricing with **actual system usage**.

---

### 3. Enterprise License (Annual)

**₹25L – ₹1Cr / year**

Includes:
- VPC / on-prem deployment
- SSO (Okta / Azure AD)
- Advanced evaluation metrics
- Compliance readiness (SOC2, audit logs)
- Priority support & SLA

---

### 4. One-Time Setup / AI Foundation Fee

**₹15L – ₹40L (one-time)**

Includes:
- Knowledge architecture design
- Chunking & ingestion strategy
- RAG evaluation framework setup
- Deployment & handover
